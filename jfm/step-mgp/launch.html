Welcome to Job Force Manager!

Job Force Manager (JFM) is a valuable tool for organizations looking to manage their pay plans, position slotting, compensation, and market data. JFM helps organizations make informed decisions about pay grades, compensation and market data by allowing them to view, adjust, and track their pay plans, individual factor scores, position slotting, grade order list, employee data, peer organizations, and market data. By using JFM, organizations can ensure that their compensation packages are competitive and in line with the market, leading to increased job satisfaction and employee retention. Additionally, JFM's ability to easily track and manage all of this information in one centralized location saves time and increases efficiency, allowing organizations to focus on their core business goals.

JFM is an application that helps organizations determine the pay grade and market value of job positions. It includes three main menus:

• Pay Plans - The Pay Plans menu allows you to view and adjust your pay structure
• Slotting - The Slotting/Scoring menu allows you to review and adjust the individual factor scores and overall JAT scores of each classification
• Market - The Market menu enables you to review, update and add market data from peer organizations

To create and slot a new position, please follow the steps below:

1. Add the position on the "Add New Position" sheet
2. Score the position on the "JAT Scoring" sheet
3. Add any available market data on the position in the "Edit Market Data" sheet
4. Find the position in the "Position Slotting" sheet and select "Edit" to assign the position to a grade
