Welcome to Job Performance Tracker!

Job Performance Tracker offers three sections:

  1. Self Evaluation: Complete a comprehensive assessment about your performance in the previous evaluation period.
  2. Employee Evaluation: Complete a comprehensive assessment about the performance of each employee that you directly supervised in the previous evaluation period.
  3. Evaluation Review: Review the completed evaluations of the employees you supervise, both directly and indirectly. If the evaluation is still active, it can be found in the Active Evaluations section, whereas closed evaluations can be found in the Evaluation Archive section.

If your information needs to be updated to reflect changes to your class title, department, or employee(s) that you supervise, please contact your Human Resources department. If you need technical support, please email Evergreen Support.
