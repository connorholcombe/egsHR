Welcome to Job Performance Tracker!

Job Performance Tracker is designed to streamline and enhance your performance management process. With Job Performance Tracker, you can effortlessly assess and track the progress of your team members, empowering you to foster a culture of growth, engagement, and success.

Job Performance Tracker offers three distinct pages to cater to your evaluation needs. Let's take a closer look:

1. Self Evaluation: Take charge of your professional development by completing a comprehensive self-assessment. Reflect on your achievements and identify areas for improvement—the Self Evaluation page is your personal space to showcase your accomplishments and aspirations.
2. Employee Evaluation: As a supervisor, you play a crucial role in shaping your team's performance. Access the Employee Evaluation page to provide constructive feedback and evaluate your employees based on predefined criteria. Gain valuable insights into their strengths, address any challenges, and establish a framework for ongoing development.
3. Evaluation Review: Discover a consolidated view of the self-evaluations submitted by your employees and the reviews you submitted about your employees. Leverage this data to guide coaching conversations and make informed decisions about talent management.

Job Performance Tracker simplifies the evaluation process, saving you time and effort. With its user-friendly interface and robust features, you can easily navigate through the platform, complete evaluations, and access essential performance data—all in one place.

We believe that Job Performance Tracker will revolutionize the way you manage performance, fostering a culture of continuous improvement and driving organizational success. Embrace this opportunity to cultivate talent, nurture growth, and unlock the full potential of your team.

Welcome aboard the Job Performance Tracker journey. Let's shape exceptional performance together!
