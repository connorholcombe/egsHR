Page Overview

Edit

Add Employee Add an employee to Job Performance Tracker. This page will prompt you to create login credentials, provide employee data, and identify the competencies that will be used for this employee.
Employee Data Edit and review employee data such as department, class title, compensation data, and more.
Evaluation Status Change an employee's evaluation status to allow the employee and/ or supervisor to complete their evaluation. This menu will also give the option to finalize an evaluation. Finalizing an evaluation will:

1. Remove that evaluation from the Active Evaluations page and move it to the Review Evluations page
2. Scores will then be counted on the Score Summary, Succession Planning, and Potential x Performance Matrix pages
3. Allow the employee's evaluation to be opened during the next review period
Employee Competency Assignments Edit or add to the competencies that each employee has as a part of their evaluation process. An employee's competencies cannot be changed if the self or supervisor evaluations are active.

Review

Submission Status Review the submission status of those employees with open evaluations, either for themselves, their supervisors, or both. Donut charts give you a quick birds eye look, or filter the data to break down completion by class title, department, or completion status. Download the tables to have a complete list along with email addresses for the employee, supervisor, supervisor II, and supervisor III to make contacting employees quick and easy.
Score Summary Review scores for finalized evaluations. Bar and area charts give a visual representation of score distributions. Data can be filtered by department, class title, and date.
Active Evaluations Review active evaluations. This list of evaluations will include any evaluation that has not been "finalized" by a JPT Administrator.
Review Evaluations Review all evaluations that have been finalized. All evaluations are grouped by employee so that you can easily see a history of performance.
Employee Compensation Review how an employee's evaluation score might affect their compensation. The percentage adjustments are set based off of the "Compensation Grid" figures in the "System" menu.

Succession

Succession Planning Inform decisions about employee succession plans. First, turn on the succession planning option for the positions you wish to plan for. Next, select the individual to see the suggested succession candidates based on two different models of succession scores.
Potential x Performance Matrix Get a wider view of succession planning by looking at employee performance and potential. This matrix divides employees up in a classic 9-box manner to display every combination of performance and potential scores, as evaluated by the employee's supervisor.

System

Compensation Grid Update the percentage increases associated with each level of performance, as well as based on current quartile of the employee. If you do not want to differentiate by quartile or by performance level you can enter equal values in the corresponding cells. These percentages will be updated automatically on the Employee Compensation sheet.
Competencies Revise current competencies and their definitions, employees assigned these competencies will be updated in real time. Additionally, add new competencies as needed.
Deactivate Account Activate or deactivate accounts for employees who are no longer active with the organization. The most common reason to deactivate an account would be employee departure. If an employee returns to the organization after a departure, their account can be reactivated here, rather than receiving a new account.
Pay Plans Update the values of your pay plans. Employees assigned to these grades will have their min, mid, and max automatically updated.
Performance Threshold Update the JPT score thresholds for evaluations to determine what scores fall into which performance levels. These thresholds are utilized on the Potential x Performance Matrix, as well as within Employee Compensation table and the Compensation Grid
Reactivate Evaluation Reactivate mistakenly 'finalized' evaluations. This can only be done if the employee and supervisor evaluations are not currently active. If there is any data in the current employee or supervisor evaluation fields, that data will be overwritten.
