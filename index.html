The job Assessment Tool (JAT) is a survey-based approach used by Evergreen to understand the key responsibilities and requirements of a given role from the perspective of employees. This tool is designed to identify gaps in knowledge or skills required for the job and to evaluate the effectiveness of current training or onboarding processes. The insights obtained from this tool can be used to improve job descriptions and help inform compensation and classification recommendations.

Job Force Manager (JFM) is a valuable tool for organizations looking to manage their pay plans, position slotting, compensation, and market data. JFM helps organizations make informed decisions about pay grades, compensation and market data by allowing them to view, adjust, and track their pay plans, individual factor scores, position slotting, grade order list, employee data, peer organizations, and market data.
