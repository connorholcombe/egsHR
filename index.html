Evergreen Solutions partners with public sector clients to effect positive change. The Job Assessment Tool (JAT) is a survey-based approach used by Evergreen to understand the key responsibilities and requirements of a given role from the perspective of employees. The insights obtained from this tool can be used to improve job descriptions and help inform compensation and classification recommendations. Evergreen is working diligently to add a sample of the JAT to this site.

Job Force Manager (JFM) is a valuable tool for organizations looking to manage their compensation system. JFM helps organizations make informed decisions about pay grades assignments and employee compensation by allowing them to view, adjust, and track their pay plans, pay grade assignments, market data, and more.

Job Performance Tracker (JPT) is a competency-based tool for evaluating employee performance through self-assessments and evaluations by supervisors. It empowers employees to gauge their strengths, areas for improvement, and goals, while supervisors can assess progress, provide feedback, and identify developmental needs. JPT streamlines the evaluation process, fosters growth, and promotes effective performance management within the organization.
