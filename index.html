The Job Assessment Tool (JAT) is a survey-based approach used by Evergreen to understand the key responsibilities and requirements of a given role from the perspective of employees. The insights obtained from this tool can be used to improve job descriptions and help inform compensation and classification recommendations. Evergreen is working diligently to add a sample of the JAT to this site.

Job Force Manager (JFM) is a valuable tool for organizations looking to manage their pay plans, position slotting, compensation, and market data. JFM helps organizations make informed decisions about pay grades, compensation, and market data by allowing them to view, adjust, and track their pay plans, individual job factor scores, position slotting, employee data, and market data.

Performance Tracker (PT) is a competency-based tool for evaluating employee performance through self-assessments and evaluations by supervisors. It empowers employees to gauge their strengths, areas for improvement, and goals, while supervisors can assess progress, provide feedback, and identify developmental needs. PT streamlines the evaluation process, fosters growth, and promotes effective performance management within the organization.
